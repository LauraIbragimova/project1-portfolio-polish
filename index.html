Featured Project

The Policy
Nest

AI Policy Tracking for Healthcare

The Policy Nest is a lightweight AI policy tracker built for healthcare stakeholders. It monitors and summarizes AI-related policy changes across federal, state, and international jurisdictions — delivering concise impact analyses tailored for healthcare operators, payers, VBC teams, and ACO leaders who need to stay ahead of regulation without adding headcount.

It provides a full project implementation plan, pre-built alert frameworks organized by healthcare-relevant AI policy domains, policy impact summary templates, and a financial modeling plan with ROI analysis for organizations evaluating AI policy compliance investment.
